5 Advantages of Investing in Your 20s

August 19,2022

Author

Dragan Stojanovski

Most youngers who go through 20s are in the process of starting their career. During the mid-20s, most of them are relying on their families to cover up expenses. Then they complete education and get into the corporate world. Only a little number of youngers in their 20s think about investing their money. If you are in your 20s, it is quite important to pay special attention towards investing your money. That’s because investing at an early stage as in your 20s will be able to offer a large number of benefits to you. For example, it will help you with gaining financial discipline at a young age. On top of that, you will be able to stay away from numerous unexpected expenses as well. The knowledge that you gather about finances during your 20sw ill surely benefit you for the rest of your life.



Should you start a business to invest?


Investing in 20s doesn’t mean that you should be a business owner. There are numerous part-time investment opportunities available for you to consider.

Even if you decide to climb up in the corporate ladder, your knowledge on investments will be able to benefit you. For example, as you progress through your corporate career, your income will increase. The financial discipline you have will help you to ensure financial stability all throughout.

Investing is not something that you can do overnight. It is a long-term activity. You will need to study different variables and get into investing. If you start your investments in the early 20s, you will be able to have sufficient time in order to learn out of your failures.

On the other hand, you will have the flexibility to venture into different investment strategies as well. Some of the investment strategies can be riskier when compared to others. However, such riskier strategies will be in a position to deliver higher returns. You can get a first hand experience in all of them, which is quite useful.

Along with investing, you will be able to get your hands on a trading account as well. Then you will be able to use the trading account to proceed with investments from your own home or work desk. While keeping these in mind, let’s deep dive and take a look at all the prominent reasons on why you need to start your investments in 20s.



Top benefits of investing in your 20s


Let’s take a better look at some of the most benefits that you will be able to get when you start investing in your 20s. These reasons will surely convince you to go ahead with investments.



You have all the time to invest


When you want to invest your money, you need to make sure that you have sufficient time for it as well. People who are going through their 20s will have more than enough time to proceed with investments. Therefore, you don’t have to worry too much about ending up with stress of not being able to get your work done on time.

It is true that people who are in their 20s don’t have a lot of money when compared to adults. However, they are having more time than adults. It is up to them to get the most out of available time to get into investments.

To get a better understanding of this, you will need to be mindful about the concept of compounding. Compounding is the overall ability that you have in order to grow your investment, as you continue to re-invest your previous earnings. This is one of the proven methods available for you to double your money. Even Albert Einstein was impressed with the concept of compounding, and he called it as the 8th wonder that exists out there in the world.

Time plays a major role behind compounding. This is where people in their 20s will be able to take advantage of. You will be able to use time in order to reinvest your earnings. Along with that, you can end up with getting the maximum returns coming out of your investments.

For example, let’s assume that you invest just $10,000 when you are 20. Then the investment will surely grow more than $70,000 when you reach the age of 60, even if you don’t do anything special. This is calculated based on 5% rate of interest. But if you invest $10,000 when you are 30 years old, you will only be able to end up with getting a return of $43,000 when you reach the age of 60. Likewise, a person who invests $10,000 at the age of 40 will only be able to get a return of $26,000 at the age of 60. This is a classic example available to show why you should be investing your money in your early 20s.



You will be able to take more risks


Every investment comes with at least some risk. This is the main reason on why specialist investors encourage not to invest money that you can’t afford to lose. The ability you have to take more risk will be higher in your 20s. It is another major reason on why you need to think about going ahead with your investments in your 20s.

As an investor, you will not be able to make the right choice at all times. There are multiple factors, which are beyond your control as well. Due to these factors, there is a possibility for you to end up with a loss. You will be able to absorb these risks in a better way when you are going through your 20s.

When you are reaching your retirement, you will not be able to take a lot of risks, as you used to do in your early 20s. That’s because a simple mistake that you do, or a bad day might make you end up with losing all your retirement savings. To make sure that you don’t end up with any such problems, you will need to invest your money in your early 20s. Even if you end up with losing all the money you invested, there is more than enough time for you to start from the scratch and earn that amount.

People who are nearing the retirement will be focusing more on risk-free and low-risk investments. These investments are not in a position to deliver the best returns. For example, deposit certificates and bonds will only deliver small returns. When you compare your returns with inflation, you might even notice that you are not getting a decent return at all. However, you will be in a position to go ahead with taking better risks when you are in the 20s. By taking maximum advantage out of it, you should think about building an aggressive portfolio. The aggressive portfolio you build in the 20s will be associated with a higher level of volatility. However, it will also be in a position to provide higher gains to you.



You can try new things


There are things that we can learn only with first hand experience. In other words, you should try it in order to understand. During your 20s, you will be able to try out those different strategies and learn from them as well.

The main reason on why you can go ahead with a trial and error investment strategy in your early 20s is because you will have the time and flexibility to study investing. You will not always end up with success, and you will encounter failures as well. No matter what, you will be able to learn from your mistakes, and make sure that you don’t commit to the same mistakes in the future.

The overall process of investing is associated with a lengthy learning curve. Unfortunately, people who are above their 30s will not be in a position to allocate time on this lengthy learning curve. For example, they will not be able to spend few months or even years to do something. However, this story is quite different for the individuals who are going through their 20s. It is even possible for them to take their time to learn more about markets. Based on lessons learns, the young investors will be able to refine their investment strategies. It can eventually help them to get maximum returns out of their investments.

All in all, young investors who are going through their 20s are in a position to absorb the risk that they have to face. Moreover, it will be possible for them to overcome the mistakes that they would do with investments. That’s because they have all the time needed to recover from their mistakes. While keeping this in mind, you may venture into investments as well.



You are tech savvy


People who are in their 20s were born with technology. In other words, they are more tech savvy when compared to the previous generation. Along with the help of technology, it is possible to venture into multiple investment opportunities. Individuals who are in their 20s have the maximum potential to reap those benefits.

For example, along with the development of Blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies, a large number of new investment opportunities were created. If you want to get into these investments, you need to be tech-savvy. However, we often see how a lot of adults and seniors getting into cryptocurrency and Blockchain investments. It all looks like rocket science to them as they are not familiar with technology. But people who are in their 20s will be able to understand what these technologies are all about and how to get their hands on such investments.

The same is applicable for online trading platforms as well. For example, the online trading platforms can be quite useful for technical analysis as well as fundamental analysis. Likewise, there are educational websites, financial websites, and chatrooms, which the investors can use to gather useful knowledge.

Some other technologies that are available for the investors to take advantage of including social media and mobile apps. People who are familiar with them will be able to overcome the learning curve and directly get their hands on investments. This is why the youngsters who are in their 20s should invest with technology. Then you will be able to use your expertise, confidence, experience, and knowledge to get the most out of your investments.



You can reach financial stability fast


All of us are looking forward to achieving financial stability. If we can achieve financial stability fast, we will be able to enjoy the rest of our lives with doing things that we love to do. For example, we will have more time to spend with our families. If you want to achieve financial independence fast, you will need to get your hands on investments during your 20s. A job will not be able to help you with achieving financial independence unless you are super-talented to climb up in the career ladder fast. However, the right investment can help you with achieving financial independence within a couple of years. Imagine how your life would be if you can achieve financial independence when you reach the year 30. Then you can spend the next years of your life to travel the world with your loved ones or do anything that you want. Hence, the decision that you take to invest in your 20s will not be something that you will regret about.



Final words


Now you are aware about 5 of the best reasons on why you should get into investing money during your 20s. There are multiple investment opportunities available for the individuals who are in their 20s as well. It is better if you can take you time to learn more about those different investment opportunities. Then you will be able to proceed with investing on the right opportunities, which can help you with experiencing all the benefits that we mentioned above.

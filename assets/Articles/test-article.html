Top 5 Money Transfer Apps for 2022

Date May 27,2022

Author

Dragan Stojanovski

Long gone are the days of sending a cheque. Transferring money in 2022 is easy peasy. Let’s look into the best money transfer apps for 2022.

Money transfer apps make it easy for people to send money domestically and overseas electronically.

They allow third-party companies to move money from one place to another, similar to a bank.

They are safe and fast – the best money transfer apps can deliver your funds within one business day.

Money transfer apps allow you to painlessly send money from one country to another using different currencies.

Transfer app companies make their money by either charging a transaction fee or through the spread.

The spread is the difference between the price you are charged for the transfer and the price it costs the company to conduct the transaction.

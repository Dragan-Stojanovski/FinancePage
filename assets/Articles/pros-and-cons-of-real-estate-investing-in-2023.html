Pros and Cons of Real Estate Investing in 2023

August 19,2022

Author

Ilija Blazhevski

Are you looking forward to getting your hands in real estate investing in 2023? Then it is better if you can evaluate both pros and cons associated with it.

Based on pros and cons, you can decide whether real estate investing is the right thing to do in 2023 or not.

The global pandemic created a temporary damper within the housing market. However, now we can see how the real estate market is picking up.

Hence, you might feel like it is a good idea to invest on real estate in 2024. To make your life easy, we will deep dive into the facts and confirm whether you should really get into real estate investing or not.

Pros of real estate investing in 2023

As the first thing, let’s take a look at the pros associated with real estate investing in 2023. These are all the good reasons on why you should invest in real estate.



Real estate prices will not reach the 2021 highs in 2023


According to the stats published by Zillow, the growth of home value appreciation was 19.5% back in the year 2021. However, it dropped up to 11% in the year 2022. Even as of now, it is on the lower side. As per what we can see, it will not reach the 2021 highs in 2023. This will provide you with a great reason to get your hands on real estate investments for the year 2023.

We can see how people who are looking forward to purchasing new homes are taking advantage out of this situation as of now. It is up to you to take advantage out of the situation as a real estate investor. You will not have to spend a considerable amount of money as you had to do in the year 2021.



More people are looking forward to relocating


The demand for property is expected to increase in 2023. That’s because most people are now looking forward to relocating.

According to the recent statistics, around 56% people in the United States have thought about moving back in the year 2021. It was just 35% back in the year 2020. The interest among people to relocate is going to increase. This can create a favorable situation for the people who are about to invest their money in real estate.

However, you will need to do a bit of a research and then discover what areas are heavily in demand among people who are looking forward to relocating.

For example, most people are now working remotely, and they are looking forward to relocating to remote areas. However, those people are still looking for excellent infrastructure as well. Therefore, you need to understand the preferences of people who are about to relocate and then invest your money on right investments. Then you can expect to get the best returns coming on your way.



Interest rates will be low in 2023 as well


Interest rates are now at a very low level. It is true that experts predict a hike in the interest rates in the near future.

However, we cannot expect the interest rates to climb all of a sudden. It will slowly crawl up. However, the interest rates for the year 2023 will still remain low. This provides you with a great reason to invest in real estate.

The Federal Reserve has made an official announcement, stating that it is looking forward to holding the federal funds at a rate of 0% - 0.25%. They will continue to do it until they can see some progress in the labor market.

Moreover, they are waiting for a situation where the long-term inflation outlook would increase. We cannot expect that to happen in the year 2023. Hence, the Federal Reserve will not have any valid reason to increase the interest rates.

Once the Federal Reserve moves the federal fund rate, it may create an impact on the 10-year treasure. If that happens, the fixed rate mortgage will go up accordingly. Make sure that you don’t wait till 2024, where an interest rate hike is about to happen.



The demand for rental property will increase


Another great reason on why you should invest in real estate for the year 2023 is because the demand for rental property would increase. Most real estate investors are looking forward to making money out of rental property. If you are one of them, you should go ahead and invest accordingly.

We can see how the increasing home value is creating an impact on the rental market. Due to increasing home prices, most people are now looking forward to staying at rental properties.

On the other hand, property owners are trying to sell their property and take advantage over the increasing home prices. This creates an ideal environment for a real estate investor who wishes to invest money in a property and rent it out for tenants.

Saving for hefty down payments is a major challenge that most property owners have to face. In such situations, the next best option available for them is to stay at a rented property and start saving money. The demand for rental property would surely increase in 2023, and you will be able to take maximum benefits that come along with it by investing in real estate.



Rents are sky high


This benefit is linked with the previous benefit. Since more people are looking forward to rent property, the rents are usually sky high as of now.

We can expect the rents to further increase in the future. The supply is really low for rented properties when compared to the demand. Hence, there is no other option available for the landlords other than to increase the rent. You can think about securing better rewards out of this by making the right investments.

Before you invest in real estate for renting, do a bit of a research and understand whether the property that you are about to get is desirable for people who are willing to rent. If you can go for a property in the right location with right amenities, you will surely be able to rent it out for a great price. The amount of time and effort you spend on this background research is totally worth when compared to the long-term benefits that you can receive.



The overall real estate market is stable


Getting into real estate investments in an unstable market can be quite risky. However, you don’t have to deal with any such risk because the real estate market is quite stable as of now. The best thing about real estate market as of now is that it is not driven by the real estate investors. Things would remain pretty much the same for the year 2023 as well.

Bad mortgages and real estate flippers would surely come into play in 2023. However, things will not turn bad in the same year. When compared to them, there’s a large number of legitimate buyers out there. Therefore, you will not have to worry about any risk caused by



A price drop will not happen in 2023


Regardless of the slight increases that we can see in the interest rates, and the reduction of inventory, we cannot expect for a price drop to happen in 2023. Therefore, you don’t need to keep a lot of doubts in mind when you are investing in real estate. Just keep the peace of mind, do your homework, and then proceed with the investments.

Some of the expert real estate investors say that nobody is aware about the future changes of housing market. But if you take a look at the reports, you will be able to get a better understanding. Real estate is an asset in demand as of now.

Hence, there is no valid reason for the market to go down at all. The market might slow down towards the end of the year 2023. However, we are pretty confident that it will not fall by the end of the year. The main reason why the market would slow down is because the builders have not constructed enough houses to cater the demand. Moreover, the interest rates would still remain low, which enhances the purchasing power of people.

Cons of real estate investing in 2023

Now you are aware about the reasons to get your hands on real estate investing in 2023. Similar to these benefits, there are some drawbacks as well. Let’s take a look at those drawbacks.



You will have to deal with competition


Most real estate investors are aware of the fact that 2023 is going to be a good year for them.

As a result, they are looking forward to taking maximum benefits out of market conditions in the year. On the other hand, we can also expect a lot of people to become new real estate investors, just because of the favorable market conditions that would exist.

Due to these reasons, the competition for real estate investments would surely increase. If you have any plans to get your hands on any investment, you should be mindful about this.

It is better if you can make yourself prepared to face bidding wars. We could see how a hype was created for the real estate industry back in the month April 2022. In fact, around 75% of the offers made were associated with bidding wars. This was just under 60% in the previous year. This trend will continue in the upcoming year as well. If you are not ready to face the situation, you will fail to get the most out of market conditions. Hence, make sure that you are fully prepared before getting your hands on real estate investments.



It can be a seller’s market


Some real estate experts predict that 2023 will be a seller’s market. A seller’s market can surely be unfortunate for all buyers out there. After many years, we can see how there is a massive demand for real estate, and a very low supply. Some people are now returning back to office from remote work. As a result, in urban areas, we can see how a massive demand exist for properties. However, the supply is not in a position to cater to this demand. This would eventually contribute towards the creation of a seller’s market at the end of the day.

According to a research done by Zillow in the recent past, there is a shortfall in around 1.4 million new homes as of now. It all happened due to construction slowdown, which initiated back in the year 2008. Supply chain issues in the past couple of years hampered the overall building process as well.


Final Words


Now you have a clear idea about the pros and cons of getting into real estate investments in the year 2023. By evaluating these pros and cons, we encourage you to decide whether real estate investing in 2023 makes sense for you.

You can also do a bit of research on your own. For example, you can go through some economic reports. Then you can get some valuable inputs, which will help you with deciding whether you should make a move or not. In case if you are in a position to overcome the challenges we mentioned above, investing in real estate can be a good decision to take. Then you will be able to get the most out of benefits that we highlighted above.

The best market conditions are there for real estate investors who are looking forward to buying property and rent them. Even if you are not, you may evaluate the pros and cons of different investment methods on your own and make the right choice. No matter what, make sure that you don’t rush your decisions. By taking time, it is possible for you to end up with securing the best results at the end of the day.
